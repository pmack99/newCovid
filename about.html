About me
My name is Preston Mack. Although I have been a professional photogrpaher for 25 years, I have always loved computers and software development. From 2007-2010, I was an Apple Certified Developer and created 4 iOS games. In 2019 I graduated from the UCF Coding Bootcamp where I learned the tools of a web developer. When the 2020 COVID-19 Pandemic struck, I was inspired to create a site that would help me to visualize all this amazing data.
